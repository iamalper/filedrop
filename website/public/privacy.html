Weep Transfer Privacy Policy

App (Weep Transfer) respects privacy and don't collect any personal information.

When app crashs or any unexpected behavior happens, will send logs to "Google Crashlytics" for help me for improving app stability.

Logs include your device specifies (such as screen size, Android version etc.) filenames and timestamps but they don't include any personal or sensitive information.

App not expected to connect internet or any servers, all work done in local network.

App will request storage permissions to download incoming files then notifies the user about files. Users can disable the permission in device settings, but app will become unable to download files.

Users shouldn't use the app in untrusted networks. App couldn't verify the safety of incoming files

If any changes made in this privacy policy, users will be notified via update notes. It's your responsibility to check update notes

For any questions or data delete requests please contact at email in the App Store page.
